# Support

## Where to get help

- **Bugs and feature requests:** [GitHub Issues](../../issues)
- **Discussion and community support:** [Discord](https://discord.gg/GfU5ypAbaD)
- **Private or security-sensitive contact:** email `alex@cloudcell.nz`

## Before you open an issue

- Check [KNOWN_ISSUES.md](KNOWN_ISSUES.md) to see if the problem is already listed.
- Include your operating system and the version of the preview build.
- Describe the steps to reproduce the issue.
